About us

Welcome to Sellsta, your number one source for all things shopping. We're dedicated to providing you the very best products, with a focus on quality, customer service, and reliability. Founded in 2010, Sellsta has come a long way from its beginnings in Akihabara. When we first started out, our passion for electronics drove us to start our own business. We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Our mission at Sellsta is to create a seamless and enjoyable shopping experience for our customers. We are constantly expanding our product range and enhancing our services to meet the diverse needs of our customers. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to ensure that every purchase you make with us meets your expectations, offering you both convenience and satisfaction in every transaction. Thank you for choosing Sellsta as your preferred shopping destination.
